# Code of Conduct

This repository is intended for professional collaboration around infrastructure, Kubernetes, and AI platform operations.

## Standards

- Be respectful and direct.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Share sanitized technical evidence.
- Avoid exposing customer-sensitive details.
- Assume good intent while still reviewing carefully.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Publishing credentials or customer-sensitive information
- Harassment or personal attacks
- Deliberately disruptive comments or issues
- Misrepresenting untested commands as validated production steps

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, close, or remove issues and pull requests that expose sensitive data or break the collaboration standards above.
